ABOUT

Introduction:

Hello there! My name is Tanja, and at 35 years old, I bring a diverse background to the table.

Formerly, I worked as a nurse specializing in emergency care, but currently, I'm embarking on a new journey as a student at Haaga-Helia University of Applied Sciences, studying to become an IT specialist.

My passion for learning is insatiable, and I'm particularly drawn to energy work and the exploration of energies.

In my spare time, you'll often find me immersed in various activities.

I have a contagious laughter that accompanies me wherever I go. I love the outdoors, whether it's cycling, swimming, hiking, or running through the forest trails.

Winters are reserved for skiing down snowy slopes, a favorite pastime of mine.


Interacting with people is something I excel at; I'm equally comfortable working collaboratively or independently.

When I need to recharge, you'll likely find me in the forest or utilizing my energy healing abilities, either alone or with friends.

Among my hobbies, you'll find me hitting the gym, particularly enjoying body combat group fitness classes.


I also have a passion for teaching, currently instructing coding to children aged 9-14.

Additionally, I work in a grocery store, stocking shelves and assisting customers.

My professional journey has been varied, including entrepreneurship, ambulance work, various retail positions, coaching, and judging gymnastics for several years.

I've also held leadership roles in retail settings and served as a head coach.

Ultimately, my aspirations involve becoming a proficient coder, creating applications, websites, animations, and perhaps even games.

I aim to lead both myself and others, engaging in meaningful work while embracing life to the fullest.

In essence, I thrive on embracing new challenges, connecting with others, and cultivating a life rich in learning, growth, and meaningful experiences.
